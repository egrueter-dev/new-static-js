The netzke-basepack gem was updated to 0.3.0, introducing the BasicApp widget: a basis for a Ext.Viewport-based widget (“application”).

Currently it supports:

  • dynamic loading of widgets
  • authentification support
  • restoring the last loaded widget (on the per user basis)
  • browser history support
  • FeedbackGhost-widget powered feedback

See the live demo on http://netzke-demo.writelesscode.com/basic_app/intro
